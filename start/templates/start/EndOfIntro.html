You have now reached the end of the practice rounds. In the following you will take turns and either play the educator who has to tell the teacher how many marbles the children have found, or the teacher who has to buy sweets for the children. You will play 3 times the educator, then 5 times the teacher, then, again, the educator, etc. In all you have to describe 21 times, and buy sweets 35 times.

Remember that the following was promised:

- a piece of chocolate to the children who find all of their 4 marbles

- a piece of hard candy to the children who find between 1 and 3 of their 4 marbles

- a gummy bear to the children who find none of their 4 marbles

Only sweets that are need to reward the children should be bought.
